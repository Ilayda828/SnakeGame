## How to Play

W - Move up
A - Move left
S - Move down
D - Move right

## Objective

Control the snake to eat food ('F')
Avoid hitting walls or your own body
Each food eaten increases your score by 10 points

## Game Elements

O - Snake head
o - Snake body
F - Food
# - Walls
